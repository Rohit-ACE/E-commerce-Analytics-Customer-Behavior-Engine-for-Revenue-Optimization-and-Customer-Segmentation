In this Project , you will get  the Full control or Overview of " Revenue & Profit Trend ", " Costomer  Behaviour " and " Product LeveL Performace View "

***
📊 Project Overview
---
This project is based on analyzing e-commerce data to understand sales and customer behavior. In this project, data is cleaned and analyzed using SQL, and results are shown using Power BI dashboards. It helps in identifying sales trends, important customers, and product performance. Overall, the project shows how data can be used to support better business decisions.

---
 - Executive Summary Page
   - in Executive summary is a kind of summary page where you will the overall Values , get to know  where and how much Bussiness is  Grwoing
- Trend insights page
   - In this page , you will the full Trend view From Year to Hour , with respect  to all impoertant Bussiness Metrices
- Product Level  page
